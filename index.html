About MAIT-V8

We are a team of educators and technologists from Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, dedicated to transforming the educational landscape. Our multimedia books integrate augmented and virtual reality to create immersive learning experiences for school students.

AR/VR Enabled Books

Our books use the latest AR/VR technologies to provide an interactive and engaging learning experience.

Interactive Learning

Students can interact with 3D models and animations, making learning more fun and effective.

Sustainable Education

By reducing paper prints, we contribute to environmental sustainability and promote eco-friendly practices.
